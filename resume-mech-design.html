Manual Robotics - Remote Operated Robot

After learning the basics of manual robots like various motors, gears and switches, we built the robot in two days working as a team of three, in November 2019 at the Manual Robotics Workshop conducted by Robocell, CCA, with the help of senior members of CCA.

  • Took the necessary measurements for clamps and holders and fixed them in place for the motors.
  • Installed wire connections from control hub of the remote to the motors.
  • Tuned the positions of worm & spur gears to reduce noise while working.

Collaborators:

  • Deb Argha Saha (myself)
  • Jugantar Mukherjee
  • Koustav Sanyal
